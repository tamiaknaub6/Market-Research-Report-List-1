<p><strong>The Automotive Night Vision Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Far Infrared (FIR) Automotive Night Vision Sensors</li><li>Near Infrared (NIR) Automotive Night Vision Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive night vision sensors market is segmented into two primary types: Far Infrared (FIR) and Near Infrared (NIR) sensors. FIR sensors detect heat emitted by objects, enabling the identification of pedestrians and animals in complete darkness, significantly enhancing safety. In contrast, NIR sensors utilize reflected infrared light, improving visibility in low-light conditions and assisting with distance perception. Both technologies aim to improve nighttime driving safety and overall vehicle performance, catering to increasing consumer demand for advanced driver-assistance systems.</p></p>
