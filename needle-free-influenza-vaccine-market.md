<p><p>The needle-free influenza vaccine market primarily includes two types: Live Attenuated Influenza Vaccine (LAIV) and Split Influenza Vaccine. LAIV is a nasal spray vaccine that uses weakened viruses to stimulate an immune response without causing illness, suitable for healthy individuals aged 2 to 49. Conversely, Split Influenza Vaccine involves inactivated virus particles and is typically administered via a jet injector, offering a safer option particularly for young children, the elderly, and those with compromised immune systems. Both types aim to enhance vaccination accessibility and comfort.</p></p>
