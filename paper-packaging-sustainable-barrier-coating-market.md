PVOH (Polyvinyl Alcohol) provides excellent oxygen and aroma barriers, ideal for food packaging. EVOH (Ethylene Vinyl Alcohol) offers superior gas barrier properties, suitable for perishable goods. PLA (Polylactic Acid) is biodegradable, derived from renewable resources, and used for compostable packaging. Other coatings include bio-based or water-based alternatives, enhancing sustainability. These materials reduce environmental impact while maintaining functionality in paper-based packaging applications.</p></p>
