<p><strong>The Automotive Wind Tunnel Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Closed Circuit</li><li>Open Circuit</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive wind tunnel market comprises two main types: closed-circuit and open-circuit. Closed-circuit wind tunnels recirculate air within a sealed loop, offering precise control over airflow conditions, making them ideal for detailed aerodynamic testing and research. Open-circuit wind tunnels draw in fresh air and exhaust it after testing, providing simpler, cost-effective solutions for basic aerodynamic evaluations. Both types are essential for vehicle development, with closed circuits favored for advanced studies and open circuits for initial testing phases.</p></p>
