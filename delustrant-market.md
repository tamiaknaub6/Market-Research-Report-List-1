<p><p>Delustrant markets are categorized into organic and inorganic types based on their chemical composition and applications in textiles and plastics. Organic delustrants, derived from carbon-based compounds, primarily provide a matte finish by scattering light, enhancing aesthetic qualities. In contrast, inorganic delustrants consist of mineral-based substances, offering durability and thermal stability. Both types serve to reduce luster in materials, improving their appearance and functionality, and are used extensively in industries such as textiles, coatings, and plastics for varied applications.</p></p>
