<p><p>The Automatic Transmission Fluid (ATF) market primarily consists of two types: semi-synthetic and full-synthetic fluids. Semi-synthetic ATF blends petroleum-based oils with synthetic additives, offering improved performance over conventional fluids at a moderate cost. Full-synthetic ATF, on the other hand, is entirely composed of synthetic base stocks, providing superior lubrication, enhanced thermal stability, and extended service life. Both types cater to the automotive industry, addressing the need for efficient transmission performance and longevity in modern vehicles.</p></p>
