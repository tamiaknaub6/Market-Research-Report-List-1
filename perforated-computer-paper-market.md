<p><ul><li>Single Layer</li><li>Multi-layer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The perforated computer paper market is segmented into single-layer and multi-layer types. Single-layer perforated paper features one continuous sheet with pre-defined perforations for easy separation, commonly used for printing invoices or forms. In contrast, multi-layer perforated paper consists of multiple sheets glued together, allowing for simultaneous printing on multiple copies, often utilized in carbonless forms and invoices. This segmentation caters to varied printing needs across industries, enhancing efficiency and ease of use in document processing.</p></p>
