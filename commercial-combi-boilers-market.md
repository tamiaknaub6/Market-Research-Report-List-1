<p><strong>The Commercial Combi Boilers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Non-condensing Boilers</li><li>Condensing Boilers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The commercial combi boilers market is divided into two main types: non-condensing boilers and condensing boilers. Non-condensing boilers operate at lower efficiency, releasing waste heat, while condensing boilers utilize flue gas heat, leading to higher efficiency and lower emissions. Non-condensing models are typically simpler and less expensive but less environmentally friendly, whereas condensing boilers are favored for their energy savings and compliance with stricter environmental regulations. The choice between them often depends on specific heating needs and efficiency goals.</p></p>
