<p><p>The inkjet inks market is primarily categorized into three types: water-based, oil-based, and solvent-based inks. Water-based inks, known for their eco-friendliness, are widely used for indoor applications and offer vibrant colors. Oil-based inks provide durability and resistance, making them suitable for outdoor printing on various substrates. Solvent-based inks feature strong adhesion properties, catering to specialized applications like signage and banners. Each type serves distinct needs, influencing market demand based on application requirements and environmental considerations.</p></p>
