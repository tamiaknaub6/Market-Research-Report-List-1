<p><ul><li>Oil-based Soap Colorants</li><li>Water-based Soap Colorants</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The soap colorants market is segmented into oil-based and water-based types. Oil-based soap colorants are derived from oils and are ideal for use in cold process soaps, offering vibrant pigmentation and durability. They blend seamlessly with oils, enhancing the final product's aesthetics. Water-based soap colorants, on the other hand, are soluble in water, making them suitable for melt-and-pour soaps and other water-based formulations. They provide a wide range of hues but may be less stable in certain formulas compared to oil-based options.</p></p>
