<p><p>The Commercial Vehicle HVAC market includes Manual and Automatic HVAC systems. Manual HVAC systems require driver intervention to adjust temperature, fan speed, and airflow, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness. They are commonly used in budget-friendly or utility-focused vehicles. Automatic HVAC systems, on the other hand, use sensors and controls to maintain desired cabin conditions without manual input, enhancing comfort and convenience. These systems are preferred in premium or long-haul vehicles, where driver comfort and energy efficiency are prioritized. Both types cater to diverse commercial vehicle needs.</p></p>
