<p><p>Catalytic molecular sieves are categorized into A Type and X Type, distinguished by their pore sizes and adsorption properties. A Type molecular sieves have smaller pores, suited for separating molecules based on size, typically used in gas and liquid purification applications. X Type molecular sieves feature larger pores, enabling them to adsorb larger molecules and ions, effectively used in petrochemical processes and catalytic reactions. Both types enhance catalytic efficiency by selectively filtering reactants and products in various industrial applications.</p></p>
