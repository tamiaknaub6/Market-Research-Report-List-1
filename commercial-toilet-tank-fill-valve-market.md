<p><strong>The Commercial Toilet Tank Fill Valve Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Float Ball</li><li>Float Cup</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The commercial toilet tank fill valve market features two primary types: float ball and float cup. The float ball design utilizes a buoyant ball attached to a lever that regulates water flow based on the tank's water level. In contrast, the float cup design incorporates a cylindrical cup that moves up and down with the water level, controlling flow through a more compact mechanism. Both types aim to ensure efficient tank refilling while minimizing water wastage in commercial settings.</p></p>
