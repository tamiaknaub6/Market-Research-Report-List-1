<p><strong>The Photoresist Anti-reflective Coatings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bottom Anti-reflective Coatings</li><li>Top Anti-reflective Coatings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Photoresist Anti-reflective Coatings market is categorized into Bottom Anti-reflective Coatings (BARCs) and Top Anti-reflective Coatings (TARCs). BARCs are applied beneath the photoresist layer to minimize reflection from the substrate, enhancing exposure uniformity in photolithography processes. On the other hand, TARCs are placed above the photoresist to reduce light reflection, improving resolution and feature definition. Both types are critical in semiconductor manufacturing, enabling better performance and efficiency in the fabrication of integrated circuits and microelectronics.</p></p>
