<p><strong>The Automotive Tempered Glass Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Full Tempered Glass</li><li>Zone Tempered Glass</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive Tempered Glass Market includes Full Tempered Glass and Zone Tempered Glass. Full Tempered Glass is uniformly heat-treated, making it strong and shatter-resistant, ideal for side and rear windows. Zone Tempered Glass has varying strength zones, with the central area being stronger to resist impacts while the edges are softer to allow controlled breakage, commonly used for windshields. Both types enhance vehicle safety by reducing injury risks during accidents and ensuring durability under stress.</p></p>
