<p><ul><li>Single-punch</li><li>Rotary</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Lab scale tablet presses are essential for pharmaceutical research and development, primarily categorized into two types: single-punch and rotary presses. Single-punch tablet presses operate by compressing powder using a single set of punches, suitable for small-scale production and formulation testing. In contrast, rotary tablet presses feature multiple stations for simultaneous compression, making them more efficient for higher-output runs. These machines cater to different scales of production, enabling manufacturers to optimize formulation processes and improve tablet characteristics in a controlled laboratory environment.</p></p>
