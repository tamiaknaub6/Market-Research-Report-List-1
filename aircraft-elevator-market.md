<p><ul><li>Horizontal Stabilator Aircraft Elevator</li><li>Elevons Aircraft Elevator</li><li>Levcons Aircraft Elevator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aircraft elevator market includes various types such as horizontal stabilator, elevons, and levcons. Horizontal stabilators combine the horizontal stabilizer and elevator into a single control surface, enhancing aircraft efficiency and reducing drag. Elevons serve dual functions on delta-wing aircraft, controlling both pitch and roll. Levcons, or leading-edge variable camber devices, aid in pitch control while improving stall characteristics. Each type contributes uniquely to aircraft performance, stability, and maneuverability, catering to diverse design requirements in the aviation industry.</p></p>
