<p><ul><li>DLTDP</li><li>DTTDP</li><li>DSTDP</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Thioesters are categorized into three main market types: DLTDP (Dodecyl L-thio-Diphenyl Phosphate), DTTDP (Dodecyl Thio-TriPhenyl Phosphate), and DSTDP (Dodecyl Thio-DiPhenyl Phosphate). DLTDP is used primarily in industrial applications due to its stability and effectiveness as a lubricant. DTTDP, with its triaryl structure, offers enhanced thermal stability, making it suitable for high-temperature applications. DSTDP, with a diaryl configuration, provides moderate stability and is often utilized in specialized formulations, such as agricultural or pharmaceutical products.</p></p>
