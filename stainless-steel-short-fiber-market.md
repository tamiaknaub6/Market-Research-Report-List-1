<p><p>The stainless steel short fiber market is categorized mainly into S316, S316L, and S304 grades. S316 grade offers excellent corrosion resistance and is ideal for marine and chemical applications. S316L, a low-carbon version, provides enhanced weldability and is used in environments with stringent corrosion requirements. S304 grade is widely utilized due to its good durability and resistance to oxidation, making it suitable for a variety of general applications. Each grade meets different industry needs based on specific properties.</p></p>
