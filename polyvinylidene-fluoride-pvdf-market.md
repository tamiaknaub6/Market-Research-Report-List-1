The PVDF market primarily consists of two types: PVDF granules and PVDF powder. PVDF granules are often used in extrusion and molding processes, suitable for producing films, sheets, and pipes. In contrast, PVDF powder is typically employed in coatings and adhesive applications, offering enhanced flexibility and even distribution. Both forms cater to various industries, especially in chemicals, electronics, and renewable energy.</p></p>
